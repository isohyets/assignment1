Haji Lane

As one of the narrowest streets in Singapore, the 'hip' streets stands out as one of the must visits!
Located in the Kampong Glam neighbourhood, Haji Lane has created its own identity and attracts many tourists from all around the world. Haji Lane’s history begins in the 1800s when the houses were used as lodges by Haji pilgrims. In the late 20th century, the houses were used as homes for poor Malay families. Today, this buzzing enclave in eclectic Kampong Glam is peppered with cool boutiques, bars, cafés, and restaurants that rank high on the hip factor – and not to forget those beautiful murals.
